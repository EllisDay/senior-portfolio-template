Redesigning the PHS PTSA Website

The teamwork behind the long-overdue renovation of the PHS Parent Teacher Student Association website.

Finding a Client

In my sophomore year of APP Academy, one of our projects was, in groups, to create a website design for a real client. With teammates Zach Huang and Euntaek Kim, I revised aspects of the original PTSA website that seemed slightly less fresh, to say the least. In their defense, the PTSA website was probably created a long time ago. Maybe it was one of the first websites ever created.
